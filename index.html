Frequently Asked Questions

What types of financial statements does NoteFlow generate?

NoteFlow generates all four primary financial statements: Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Statement of Cash Flows (indirect method), and Statement of Stockholders' Equity. It also auto-generates comprehensive notes disclosures, supplementary schedules, and a complete audit package with cover page and table of contents.

What file formats can I import?

NoteFlow accepts CSV, TSV, and Excel (.xlsx, .xls) files. Our smart import wizard auto-detects your column layout and lets you map fields with a visual interface — no need to reformat your trial balance.

Is my client data secure?

Absolutely. All data is encrypted with AES-256-GCM at rest and TLS in transit. We use Firebase Authentication for secure login, and your encryption keys are derived from your credentials so even we cannot read your data. We never sell or share client data.

Can I use NoteFlow for compilations and reviews?

Yes. NoteFlow is designed for compilation, review, and preparation engagements. The auto-generated notes disclosures are structured to assist with GAAP compliance and can be customized for your specific engagement type (tax basis, cash basis, GAAP, IFRS). Professional judgment is still required for all final output.

Does NoteFlow support comparative (multi-period) statements?

Yes. Import current and prior year trial balances together, and NoteFlow generates side-by-side comparative statements. The cash flow statement automatically computes working capital changes between periods.

What happens after the free trial?

After 14 days, you'll be asked to select a subscription plan to continue. All your data and projects are preserved. You can export everything at any time, even if your subscription lapses.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. There are no long-term contracts. Cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll retain access through the end of your billing period.

How does the AI rewrite feature work?

NoteFlow integrates with AI to help you refine notes disclosures. You provide your own API key (stored locally, never sent to our servers), and the AI can adjust tone, expand detail, or simplify language while preserving technical accuracy.
